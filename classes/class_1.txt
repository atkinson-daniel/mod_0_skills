Class: MenuItem

Attributes:
price (float)
ingredients (array)
isSpicy (boolean)
allergens (array)

Methods:
giveTenPercentDiscount (decreases the price attribute by 10%)
makeSpicy (changes the isSpicy attribute from false to true)
makeNutFree(removes the nuts the array allergens and removes ingredient from ingredient array)
removeIngredient (removes an ingredient in the ingredients array)
addIngredient (adds a new ingredient in the ingredients array)

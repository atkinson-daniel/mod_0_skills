Class: Menu Item

Attributes:
price (float)
name (string)
ingredients (array)
isSpicy (boolean)
isVegan (boolean)

Methods:
giveTenPercentDiscount (decreases the price attribute by 10%)
makeSpicy (changes the isSpicy attribute from false to true)
makeVegan (removes animal products from ingredients array and changes the isVegan attribute to true)
changeName (updates the name attribute to a new name)

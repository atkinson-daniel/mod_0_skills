Class: Sports Teams

Attributes:
playerCount (integer)
equipmentUsed (array)
arenaType (string)
isCoed (boolean)
playerNames (array)

Methods:
replacePlayer (removes a player form the playerNames array and adds another)
changeEquipment (replaces an item in the equipmentUsed array with another item)
addNewGender (changes the isCoed from false to true)
adjustPlayerCount (adds or removes a new player)
